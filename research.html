RESEARCH PAGE

We took the inspiration from the following websites while creating our own:

1. Amazon (a) : https://www.amazon.com

- The beginning part the products page of our website was inspired from the home page of amazon website.

- We took the idea of the left and right slider buttons at the top and implemented that into our webpage.

- We also made the sliders change the background image of the first container.

- The tiled boxes containing content with a negative top margin was taken as inspiration, while implementing the same concept in our list of categories.

1. Amazon (b)

- This bottom part of the amazon home page which contained a section that links to the signin and register pages along with a section that takes the customer to the top of the page was taken as inspiration.

2. Daraz : https://www.daraz.com.np/

- The style of the product cards was inspired from Daraz. Though we have added many different elements, we tried to keep with the similar background and theme.

- The style for the discounted prices, where the original price is displayed as strikethrough text, was also inspired from Daraz.

- Likewise, when hover over the products, the box gives a slight box-shadow, this was also inspired from Daraz.

3. Similarweb : https://www.similarweb.com/

- The header for our website was inspired from a particular webpage, not an ecommerce website.

- While searching through the web, we came across this webpage that we decided to take the header style from.

- Everything for the header was inspired from this page's header. We only managed to completely change the navigation area.

4. Target : https://www.target.com/

- The footer for our website was inspired from the footer of target.

- In fact, most of the links in the footer that we used have similar names to that of the target webpage.

- We managed to add some extra social site links and a copyright information at the bottom.

5. Mechmotive : https://mechomotive.com/importance-of-technology-and-its-aspects/

- The idead of a basic blog page was taken from an online blogpage on the importance of technology.

- What we did different was that, we added our own section to the right which contained links for reaching different topics and subtopic in the blog quickly.
